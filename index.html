THE PLATFORM
Why Aero Store Exists
We built Aero Store because Indian developers deserve a fair, transparent, and secure marketplace to ship their apps — without gatekeeping, bloatware, or hidden fees.
🛡️
Verified Developers Only
Every developer goes through identity and address verification. No anonymous uploads, no shady accounts. Every app is traceable to a real person or company.
🔍
Automated Malware Scanning
Every uploaded APK passes through multi-layer security scans before going live. Malicious or suspicious code is flagged and blocked instantly.
⚖️
Fair Dispute Resolution
Users and developers get unique dispute codes. Every complaint is tracked transparently with timestamped audit trails — no black-box decisions.
📊
Real Ratings & Reviews
No fake reviews. Our rating system is bot-protected and verified — only real users who downloaded the app can leave feedback.
🔒
Privacy by Default
We never sell user data. All data is encrypted, hosted on Indian servers, and auto-purged after inactivity. GDPR & Indian IT Act compliant.
🚀
Zero Bloatware Policy
Unlike mainstream stores, we don't bundle unnecessary apps, push ads into your notification bar, or auto-install anything. Clean installs only.

ARCHITECTURE
Built Different. Engineered to Last.
The technology stack and principles that power everything at Aero Store.
🤖
AI-Powered Moderation
Powered by Google Gemini — our AI reviews app descriptions, scans metadata, and auto-flags policy violations before human review.
🇮🇳
Indian-First Infrastructure
All servers run on Indian cloud regions. Optimized for Indian network conditions including 2G/3G fallback. Supports Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and more.
⚡
Fix Engine (Self-Healing)
Our proprietary monitoring system watches the platform 24/7 and auto-patches issues. Zero downtime deployments ensure users always get the best experience.
🔗
GitHub Sync for Developers
Connect your GitHub repo and auto-publish new releases. CI/CD pipeline generates specifications and changelogs from your commits using AI.
🎫
Built-in Ticketing System
No external email management needed. Users, developers, and admins communicate through an integrated support system — like GitHub Issues, but simpler.
