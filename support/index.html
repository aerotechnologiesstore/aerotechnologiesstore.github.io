How Live Support Works

Our live chat system is designed for fast resolution:

  • Start a Chat — Click the button below to instantly open a live connection.
  • Strike AI Assistance — Strike AI will answer immediately and try to resolve your issue.
  • Human Escalation — If Strike AI can't help, it instantly transfers your chat to our Aero Store team.
  • Persistent History — The human agent sees the full chat history, so you never have to repeat yourself.
02
⏱️

Response Times

  • Strike AI — Instant Response (24/7)
  • Critical Human Escalation — Within 2 hours
  • Standard Human Support — Within 12-24 hours

If an agent is currently online, you may be connected immediately upon escalation.

03
🤖

Strike AI Assistant

When you start a chat, you are instantly connected to Strike AI, which is fully trained to help with:

  • Password resets and account recovery
  • App installation troubleshooting
  • Developer dashboard navigation
  • Understanding platform policies
  • APK upload error diagnostics

If Strike AI cannot resolve your issue, it automatically routes your active chat to a human agent without requiring you to submit any email or form.

⚖️

Dispute Resolution

For disputes between users and developers:

  • Both parties receive a unique dispute code for tracking.
  • Each side has 72 hours to present their case with supporting evidence.
  • Aero Store mediates with a binding decision within 7 business days.
  • All actions are logged in a tamper-proof audit trail.
